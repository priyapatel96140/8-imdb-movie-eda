# Business Recommendations

## 1. Focus on High-Demand Genres

Action, Drama, and Comedy movies appear most frequently in the dataset. Production companies should continue investing in these genres as they consistently attract audiences.


## 2. Prioritize Quality Content

Movies with higher ratings generally tend to achieve better commercial performance. Studios should focus on strong storytelling, production quality, and audience satisfaction.


## 3. Increase Audience Engagement

A positive relationship exists between votes and revenue. Marketing campaigns, trailers, social media promotions, and audience interaction can help increase visibility and revenue.


## 4. Collaborate with High-Performing Directors

Directors with consistently high average ratings should be prioritized for future projects as they have a proven track record of delivering successful movies.


## 5. Avoid Unnecessary Runtime Expansion

Longer movies do not necessarily receive better ratings or generate higher revenue. Movie runtime should be optimized based on content requirements rather than duration alone.


## 6. Study Successful Release Years

Certain years generated significantly higher revenue and audience engagement. Analyzing the factors behind those successful years can help improve future release strategies.


## 7. Use Ratings for Content Segmentation

Classifying movies into Excellent, Good, and Average categories can support recommendation systems and help target different audience groups more effectively.


## 8. Monitor Critic and Audience Feedback

Ratings and Metascores provide important indicators of movie quality. Combining both measures can help studios better understand audience preferences and critical reception.
